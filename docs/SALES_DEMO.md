# Spendsnap Synthetic Sales Demo

Use only a dedicated synthetic company, users, receipts, vendors, GSTINs, projects, and accounting ledgers. Never reuse customer documents or production credentials.

## Audience

Finance manager, founder, accountant, or operations lead at an Indian company with recurring employee travel or field expenses.

## Fifteen-minute flow

1. **Current pain — 1 minute**
   Show the synthetic spreadsheet, receipt folder, approval messages, and Tally re-entry that Spendsnap replaces. State the measured baseline rather than generic claims.

2. **Employee capture — 2 minutes**
   Sign in as an employee, upload a synthetic receipt, show private signed storage and extraction status. Explain that critical fields are human-resolved rather than trusted because a model is confident.

3. **Claim and report — 2 minutes**
   Create a claim with business purpose/category, assemble a report, and demonstrate exact multi-currency totals and optimistic version conflicts.

4. **Policy and exception — 2 minutes**
   Preview deterministic rules, show an explainable meal limit and weekend-note warning, then request an exception. Emphasize that AI does not reinterpret company policy.

5. **Authenticated approval — 2 minutes**
   Sign in as manager, inspect the immutable submission, request changes or approve. Show self-approval protection and finance-stage assignment.

6. **Finance and GST readiness — 3 minutes**
   Sign in to the finance console, show GST completeness with cautious wording, accounting mappings, period lock, deterministic CSV export, checksum, and immutable history.

7. **Administration and advisor — 2 minutes**
   Show digest-only invitations, role controls, audit export, then one advisory suggestion. Accepting advice records intent but changes no financial fact automatically.

8. **Commercial close — 1 minute**
   Show onboarding, usage, and plan hypotheses. Offer a defined pilot with success metrics, data-handling terms, support path, and a clear paid conversion decision.

## Never claim

- guaranteed OCR or fraud detection;
- GST input-tax-credit eligibility;
- completed production security certification;
- live Tally integration when demonstrating CSV;
- automatic reimbursement or corporate cards;
- autonomous approval, policy override, or evidence deletion;
- customer adoption, savings, or accuracy without measured evidence.

## Pilot success measures

- employee submission time;
- finance minutes per receipt/report;
- correction and clarification rate;
- approval calendar delay;
- export import success and manual changes;
- critical-field extraction accuracy;
- percentage of reports completed without spreadsheet entry;
- user trust and willingness to pay.
